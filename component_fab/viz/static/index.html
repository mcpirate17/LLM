component_fab · visual explainer

This site is a visual laboratory designed to demystify the "black box" of AI architectures. Through interactive animations and plain-language analogies, we trace the journey of tokens as they transform into memory, watch in real-time as models learn to prioritize "surprising" information, and explore the evolutionary "Hall of Fame" where only the most effective designs survive. It aspires to be a living textbook for the next generation of neural design, turning complex retrieval algebra into an intuitive, animated story of how machines remember.
